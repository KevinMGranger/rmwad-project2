Project 2 Proposal

Kevin Granger & Carl Domingo

Tiger-Finder: A social directory for accepted RIT students.

Web Services

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Scraping RIT SIS if we feel insane enough

Target Devices

The app will be developed with desktop computers as a primary target, but will have a mobile-centric focus in terms of visual design and usability.

Full list of Possible Features

  • Pull in and compare user data from social media services such as Facebook, Twitter, and Google+ (Web service requirement)
  • Find other users with similar interests, majors, etc.
  • Geolocation to find other users within the same area (HTML5 technology requirement)
  • See graphed areas that illustrate where other users are across the world (Web service and HTML5 technology)
  • Report your exact location in real-time to other users for meetups during open houses (Web service and HTML5 technology)
